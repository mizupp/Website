Charity and Faith

Poppy appeal

Raised money for poppy appeal and have done public speaking events within mosque community on the importance of Poppy Appeal and Remembrance Day.

Prostate cancer

Held and helped out with cupcake sales to raise money for men with prostate cancer; along with this I spoke to students to raise awareness regarding this cancer and encourage them to ask their male relatives to go for check-ups.

Charity walks

Taken part in various charity walks to raise awareness and money.

Macmillan cancer raise

Donated and helped out at coffee mornings to raise money for Macmillan; organised groups of women to attend these coffee mornings to strengthen local community knowledge on cancer.

Part time volunteering at Ronald Gibson house

I have volunteered part-time at Ronald Gibson house to look after elderly people suffering dementia. I was able to take many skills from volunteering.

Volunteering at other care homes

For the past 6 years, I have visited various elderly care homes to help with wrapping Christmas presents and helped out at small Christmas parties. Along with this, Christmas presents are donated to people in hospitals, by hand, every year (and some care homes that allow it).

Work with disabled children

I worked on a small project regarding attitudes to disabilities and tried to raise awareness locally. The project’s aim was to promote equality regardless of disability; I was provided a response by my previous local MP, Jane Ellison.

Homeless people and food banks

I worked with neighbours and others in my area, to provide hampers and gifts at homeless shelters, in particular for women. Along with this, I have helped with collection of food within local community that was taken to food bank; we encourage donating some every year.

Inter-faith projects

Living in the UK allows us to interact with people of different cultures and faiths; I believe it is important to reach out to as many communities as possible. Helping out at different occasions has provided me with more connections to whom I can reach out to when I also need help. One of the biggest benefits of these small contributions is the time spent with other people; each person has their own life, own story and they all want someone to hear it.

Church activities

Helping out when I can with small tasks as well as actively take part in coffee mornings at Lawrence Church. Most recently, I helped out with making cards during an arts and craft session; I was able to learn different skills from the older women and develop some new techniques of my own.

Gurdwara visits

I have had the luxury of visiting many gurdwaras in my lifetime; from a young age, my mother wanted to me to get to know as many different religions as possible and with majority of her friends being Sikh, it was more of an obligation. Recently, I visited two gurdwaras through my own decision; the whole experience was a blessing; everyone was extremely friendly and I even got to have a chat with the women who help out there whilst the other women cooked. The purpose of the visit was to make connections with the gurdwara with my local mosque to make it accessible for everyone to attend exciting events at either location..
